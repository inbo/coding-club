Yes, we can Use dataframe `fit_results` to: 1. Plot number of observations (`obs`) versus time (`year`) and *facet* by species (`scientificName`). Arrange them in two rows and two columns. As y- and x- ranges are quit different among species, check that each plot has own y- and x-scale. 3. Add fit values (`fit` column) as red dotted line. 4. Add confidence interval area (lower value: `lcl`, upper value: `ucl`) with transparency equal to 0.3 and use purple as filling color. 5. Set "Number of observations in Belgian Natura2000 protected areas" as title and "Source: GBIF" as subtitle. 7. Left align both title and subtitles (`INBOtheme` centers authomatically title and subtitles).
